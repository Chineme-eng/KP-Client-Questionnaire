Web Design Services Agreement

KP Designs — Professional Website Services  ·  New York, NY

1. Scope of Services

KP Designs ("Service Provider") agrees to design, build, and deliver a professional website based on the package and add-ons selected within this document. The agreed scope is defined by the selections made in this questionnaire. Any work outside the agreed scope will be quoted separately and requires written approval before it begins.

2. Payment Terms

The setup fee is payable as selected — either in full before work begins, or in a 50/50 split (half upfront, half on launch). Monthly retainer payments begin the month after launch and are due on the same date each month. All payments are non-refundable once work has commenced. Accepted methods: Cash App, Zelle, PayPal, Venmo, or bank transfer.

3. Timeline

Work begins once the first payment is received and this agreement is signed. Timelines stated in the selected package apply. Delays caused by the client failing to provide content, photos, or timely feedback are not the responsibility of KP Designs.

4. Revisions

The setup fee includes 2 rounds of revisions during the design phase. A revision round is a set of changes sent in one message or call. Additional rounds are billed at $50 each, due before changes are made.

5. Monthly Maintenance

The monthly retainer covers the base plan and any add-ons selected. Work exceeding included hours (e.g. more than 2 hours of content updates per month) is billed at $40/hour, communicated and approved by the client before work begins.

6. Cancellation

  • Either party may cancel with 30 days written notice via email or text.
  • Upon cancellation, KP Designs will provide all website files within 7 business days.
  • Payments more than 14 days late may result in the site being taken offline until payment is received.
  • The setup fee is non-refundable once work has begun.

7. Ownership

Once the full setup fee is paid, the client owns the website and all custom content created for it. KP Designs retains the right to display the completed site in their portfolio. Stock images and third-party tools remain subject to their own licensing terms.

8. Limitation of Liability

KP Designs is not liable for lost business or revenue resulting from website downtime or third-party service outages. Total liability shall not exceed the amount paid by the client in the 3 months preceding any claim.

9. Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of New York. Disputes shall be addressed through good-faith communication before any legal action is pursued.
